A simple, instagram feed with a shoppable click-through feature. Works with or without WooCommerce.

== Description ==

Display your Instagram posts on your website and make them click through to your product pages via hashtags. Works with or without WooCommerce.

== Installation ==

From your WordPress dashboard

1. **Visit** Plugins > Add New
2. **Search** for "Junior Instagram Feed"
3. **Activate** Junior Instagram Feed from your Plugins page
4. **Click** on settings/Instagram Feed
5. Enter you access token by following the options in the admin settings
6. Use the shortcode [juniorfeed] to display posts or select manually where you want the feed to go

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What's this plugin for? =

It's to show your Instagram posts on your website

= What else can it do? =

It will link to any of your products if you enter that product name as a hashtag when you make the post on your phone
